# TrueFit Theme — Brand & Build Brief

## What we're doing

Redesigning the TrueFit Nutrition Shopify theme toward a premium, science-backed, clean aesthetic — inspired by Momentous (livemomentous.com), IM8 Health (im8health.com), and The Absorption Company (absorbmore.com). The throughline: lots of white space, restrained color, confident typography, trust signals placed early. Premium but approachable. Never gym-bro.

## Color system

- **Navy `#0A1A4F`** — primary anchor. Used for dark bands, primary buttons, key headlines. Carries the "serious/premium" weight.
- **White `#FFFFFF`** — dominant canvas/background.
- **Light cream `#FCFCEA`** — secondary background, used in alternation with white (see rhythm rule).
- **Vanilla dark blue `#40738D`** — body text, muted labels, secondary accents.
- **Vanilla light blue `#86BCE8`** — soft accents, gradients, "New" badges.
- **Chocolate dark brown `#402020`** — footer, Chocolate-product contexts.
- **Chocolate light brown `#E0C5AC`** — warm accent on dark brown.
- **Gold `#C9A86A`** — premium accent for star ratings and "Best Seller / Best Value" badges. (Not an original brand color; added for premium pop — use sparingly.)

## Section background rhythm

Alternate section backgrounds down the page: white → cream → white → cream, with navy and brown as full-color punctuation bands between them. The alternation creates editorial rhythm without heavy dividers.

## Typography

- **Brand font: Poppins.** Use the theme's existing Poppins font setup for all text (headings and body). Do not import or load a different font family. If Poppins is already configured in the theme's settings/CSS, reference that rather than re-importing.
- **Headlines:** large, tight letter-spacing (around `-0.02em`), sentence case, medium weight (~500). Not bold/heavy.
- **Eyebrow label:** above each headline, a small eyebrow label in `#40738D`, uppercase, letter-spaced (~`0.14em`), ~11px. This eyebrow-over-headline pattern is a signature move — use it on section headers.
- **Body text:** `#40738D`, comfortable line-height (~1.6–1.7).

## Brand voice

- **Manifesto:** "In a World of Fake, Stay True."
- **Promise:** "Every Ingredient is Earned."
- **Tagline:** "Stay True."
- **Tone:** confident not cocky, warm not soft, direct not blunt, premium not precious, fit not bro.

## Hard rules

- No em dashes anywhere in copy.
- No "gains" or intensity-forward gym vocabulary. No bro-culture coding.
- Avoid generic AI-sounding phrasing.
- Keep all existing functionality intact (variant logic, subscription/ReCharge widgets, cart behavior). Restyle, don't remove.

## Working method

Work in small, reviewable chunks — one section at a time. After each change, commit and push so it can be previewed before moving on. Never publish; only push to the connected branch.
